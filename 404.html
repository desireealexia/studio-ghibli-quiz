How to Play

To play, simply select your answers to the questions presented. You'll encounter multiple-choice, true/false, and image-based questions.

Choose wisely, as you can only pick one answer per question. Once you've made your selection, proceed to the next question.

At the end, you'll receive your final score.

Good luck!
